About Prashant Cooks

Welcome to Prashant Cooks! I'm passionate about sharing delicious recipes from Nepal and around the world. My goal is to make cooking accessible, fun, and enjoyable for everyone, whether you're a beginner or an experienced chef.

My Story

Growing up in Nepal, I was surrounded by incredible flavors and traditional cooking methods. This website is my way of preserving and sharing those authentic recipes while also exploring cuisines from around the globe.

What You'll Find Here

  • Authentic Nepali recipes
  • Easy-to-follow step-by-step instructions
  • Recipes for all skill levels
  • Tips and tricks for better cooking
  • Nutritional information

My Mission

To inspire people to cook at home, try new flavors, and discover the joy of creating delicious meals from scratch. Every recipe is tested and crafted with care to ensure you get the best results in your kitchen.
